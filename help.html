Help & Troubleshooting

Quick fixes for the most common sign-in and course problems, and who to contact when you need a hand.

Common Issues

Frequently asked questions

Do I need to log in to see the bundles?

No. The 5E/4F Bundle Hub requires no login, no TMS account, and no app — bundle pages contain approved standard work only, with no patient information. You only sign in to TMS when completing assigned training.

The QR code link won't open on my phone

Make sure you are using your phone's built-in camera app (not a third-party scanner) and that you tap the notification banner that appears after scanning. If nothing appears, clean the camera lens, add more light, and hold the phone 6–12 inches from the code.

TMS says my account is locked or my passcode is wrong

Use the "Forgot Username" or "Forgot Passcode" links on the TMS sign-in page first. If you remain locked out, contact the Enterprise Service Desk (ESD) at 1-855-673-4357 or submit a ticket through the yourIT portal on a VA workstation.

I signed in but a course won't launch on my phone

Some course content requires the VA network or a desktop browser. Try the course from a VA workstation. If it still fails, note the course title and error message, then contact the ESD.

A required course is missing from my To-Do list

Assignments are managed by your service's TMS administrator. Tell your unit educator or nurse manager which course is missing; they will route the request to the TMS domain manager.

I need an accommodation to complete training

TMS content is required to meet Section 508 accessibility standards. If you encounter content you cannot access with your assistive technology, notify your unit educator and the ESD so an accessible version or alternative can be provided.
