How to Sign In to TMS 2.0

Follow these steps the first time you use the QR code. After that, signing in takes less than a minute.

On Your Phone

Signing in from a personal or VA mobile device

  1. Scan the QR code

    Open your phone's camera and point it at the QR code posted on your unit. Tap the link that appears. It brings you to this Learning Hub.

  2. Tap "Sign In to VA TMS 2.0"

    You will be taken to the official VA TMS 2.0 sign-in page at tms.va.gov.

  3. Choose your sign-in method

    Select Single Sign-On Internal (SSOi) and use your VA network credentials, or sign in with your TMS username and passcode if you have one set up.

  4. Open your To-Do list

    Your assigned and overdue training appears on your TMS home screen. Tap any item to launch it.

Note: Some TMS course content requires the VA network. If a course will not launch on your phone, complete it from a VA workstation instead.
On a Workstation

Signing in from a VA computer

  1. Go to this page or straight to TMS

    Type the Learning Hub address from the unit flyer, or go directly to tms.va.gov.

  2. Sign in with your PIV card

    With your PIV card inserted, choose the PIV/SSOi option and enter your PIN when prompted.

  3. Complete training from your To-Do list

    Workstations support all TMS content types, including courses with audio and video.
