Why This Page

About the ACM Learning Hub

This page is the single destination for the QR codes posted across Acute Care Medicine units. It gives every ACM registered nurse a consistent, accessible path to the Learning Management System (LMS) — VA Talent Management System (TMS) 2.0 — so required training is easier to find and complete.

Content on this page is owned and reviewed by ACM Nursing Education. See the Help page for questions or content update requests.
